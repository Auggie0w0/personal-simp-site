Yoo Joonghyuk

From Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

Yoo Joonghyuk is a central character from the popular manhwa 'Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint'. Known for his stoic demeanor and exceptional combat skills, he is a regressor who has lived through multiple world cycles, gaining immense experience and knowledge through each regression.

Age 28
Birthday Unknown
Role Main Character/Regressor
Personality Stoic & Determined

Character Analysis

Yoo Joonghyuk is characterized by his unwavering determination and seemingly cold exterior. As a regressor who has experienced the apocalypse multiple times, he carries the weight of countless failures and the memories of those he couldn't save. This has shaped him into a pragmatic and often ruthless individual who will do whatever it takes to achieve his goals. Despite his apparent coldness, he possesses a deep sense of responsibility and a hidden capacity for compassion that emerges throughout his journey.

Fun Facts

  • Has regressed over 1,800 times through the apocalyptic scenarios
  • Master of the Breaking the Sky Swordsmanship
  • Known as the 'Supreme King' in certain scenarios
  • Possesses extraordinary physical abilities and combat skills
  • Has a complex relationship with the protagonist, Kim Dokja
  • His determination to save the world has remained constant through all regressions
