About This Gallery

A personal collection of my favorite anime and K-pop characters

Welcome to My Simp Gallery

This website is a personal project dedicated to showcasing some of my favorite characters from anime and K-pop. Each character holds a special place in my heart for different reasons - whether it's their personality, story arc, or simply the joy they bring to my life.

Featured Characters

Izumi Miyamura

From the beautiful romance anime "Horimiya", Miyamura's character development and sweet personality make him absolutely lovable.

Akito Yamada

The titular character from "My Love Story with Yamada-kun at Lv999" whose sweet romance story and relatable gamer personality make him absolutely charming.

Lee Know (Lee Minho)

A talented member of Stray Kids whose dancing skills and playful personality make him impossible not to love.

About the Website

This website was created with love using modern web technologies. The design focuses on creating a beautiful, responsive experience that showcases these characters in the best possible way. The carousel design allows for easy navigation between characters, and each character page provides detailed information about why they're special.

Technical Features

  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Smooth carousel navigation
  • Beautiful animations and transitions
  • Modern CSS with gradient backgrounds
  • Interactive hover effects

Future Plans

I plan to continue expanding this gallery with more characters as I discover new anime and K-pop groups. Eventually, I'd love to add 3D models of these characters using Blender, as mentioned in the original concept. The website will continue to evolve and grow with my interests.
