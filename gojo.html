Gojo Satoru

From Jujutsu Kaisen

Gojo Satoru is a powerful and charismatic jujutsu sorcerer from the anime and manga series 'Jujutsu Kaisen'. As a special grade sorcerer and teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High, he is known for his exceptional abilities, including his Six Eyes and Limitless technique, making him widely regarded as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer in the world.

Age 28
Birthday December 7
Role Special Grade Sorcerer & Teacher
Personality Confident, Playful & Eccentric

Character Analysis

Gojo Satoru embodies the perfect balance of overwhelming power and carefree attitude. Despite his seemingly aloof personality, he deeply cares about his students and the future of jujutsu society. His character represents the pinnacle of jujutsu sorcery while also serving as a mentor figure who challenges the corrupt hierarchical system. His iconic blindfold, white hair, and blue eyes have made him one of the most recognizable and popular characters in modern anime.

Fun Facts

  • Possesses the Six Eyes and Limitless Technique, making him exceptionally powerful
  • Always wears a blindfold or sunglasses to control his Six Eyes' constant intake of information
  • Has a sweet tooth and is often seen enjoying junk food
  • Birthday: December 7
  • His favorite phrase is 'Throughout Heaven and Earth, I alone am the honored one'
  • Known for his playful and sometimes childish personality despite his immense power
