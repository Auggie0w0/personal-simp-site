Cheongmyeong

From Return of the Blossoming Blade

Cheongmyeong is the main protagonist of 'Return of the Blossoming Blade' (also known as 'Return of the Mount Hua Sect'). As the last disciple of the once-great Mount Hua Sect, he returns to the world after being reincarnated 100 years after his death to restore his sect to its former glory. Known as the Plum Blossom Sword Saint in his previous life, he combines unparalleled swordsmanship with a mischievous personality.

Age 22
Birthday Unknown
Role Martial Artist & Sect Leader
Personality Confident, Mischievous & Strategic

Character Analysis

Cheongmyeong represents the perfect blend of genius and hard work. Despite his often playful and seemingly arrogant demeanor, he harbors a deep sense of responsibility toward reviving the Mount Hua Sect. His character journey showcases themes of legacy, redemption, and the burden of expectations. What makes him particularly compelling is the contrast between his carefree attitude and the serious weight of his mission. His growth throughout the series demonstrates how he balances honoring tradition while forging his own path forward.

Fun Facts

  • Was known as the Plum Blossom Sword Saint in his previous life
  • Reincarnated 100 years after his death to restore the Mount Hua Sect
  • Has a signature plum blossom sword technique
  • Often pretends to be weaker than he actually is
  • Has a fondness for alcohol and gambling
  • Known for his witty remarks and trolling his opponents
  • His hair color changed from black to white after his reincarnation
