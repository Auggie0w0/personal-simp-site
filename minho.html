Lee Know (Lee Minho)

From Stray Kids

Lee Know, also known as Lee Minho, is a member of the K-pop group Stray Kids. Known for his incredible dancing skills, unique personality, and love for cats, he has captured the hearts of fans worldwide with his talent and charm.

Age 24
Role Main Dancer
Personality Playful

Character Analysis

Lee Know is known for his exceptional dancing abilities and his unique sense of humor. His love for cats and his playful personality make him incredibly endearing to fans. He's also known for his dedication to his craft and his supportive nature towards his fellow group members.
