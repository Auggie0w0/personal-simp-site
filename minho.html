Lee Know (Lee Minho)

From Stray Kids

Lee Know, also known as Lee Minho, is a member of the K-pop group Stray Kids. Known for his incredible dancing skills, unique personality, and love for cats, he has captured the hearts of fans worldwide with his talent and charm. His stage name "Lee Know" reflects his desire to continuously learn and grow as an artist.

Age 26
Birthday October 25, 1998
Role Main Dancer
Personality Playful & Dedicated

Character Analysis

Lee Know is known for his exceptional dancing abilities and his unique sense of humor. His love for cats and his playful personality make him incredibly endearing to fans. He's also known for his dedication to his craft and his supportive nature towards his fellow group members. His journey from a backup dancer to a main dancer in Stray Kids showcases his hard work and determination.

Fun Facts

  • Birthday: October 25, 1998 (age ~26 in 2025)
  • Role: Main dancer and visual of Stray Kids; also contributes as a rapper/vocalist
  • Personality: Charismatic, disciplined performer; caring, "hyung" presence; well-trained in martial arts and dance
