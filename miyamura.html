Izumi Miyamura

From Horimiya

Izumi Miyamura is the male protagonist of "Horimiya", a beautiful romance anime and manga series. He's a quiet, mysterious student who hides his true self behind glasses and long hair. His relationship with Kyoko Hori reveals his sweet, caring personality and helps him open up to others.

Age 17
Role Male Protagonist
Personality Sweet & Caring

Character Analysis

Miyamura's character is defined by his dual nature - the quiet, mysterious student at school versus his true self with piercings and tattoos. His journey focuses on self-acceptance and learning to be comfortable with who he really is. His relationship with Hori helps him break out of his shell and form meaningful connections.

Why I Love This Character

  • Complex character development
  • Sweet and caring personality
  • Beautiful romance story
  • Relatable struggles with self-identity
  • Amazing character growth arc
