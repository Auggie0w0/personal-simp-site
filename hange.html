Hange Zoe

From Attack on Titan

Hange Zoe is a brilliant and eccentric scientist from "Attack on Titan", serving as the 14th Commander of the Survey Corps. Known for their passionate obsession with Titans and groundbreaking research, Hange is both a tactical genius and a compassionate leader. Their gender-neutral presentation and unique personality make them one of the most beloved characters in the series.

Age 29
Role Commander
Personality Eccentric & Brilliant

Character Analysis

Hange Zoe represents the perfect balance of scientific curiosity and human compassion in the dark world of Attack on Titan. Their journey from a passionate researcher to the Commander of the Survey Corps showcases their growth and leadership abilities. Hange's unique approach to understanding Titans through empathy and research rather than pure hatred sets them apart from other characters. Their eccentric personality, often expressed through their obsession with Titans and scientific experiments, adds much-needed humor to the series while never diminishing their competence or dedication to humanity's survival.

Why I Love This Character

  • Brilliant scientific mind and innovative thinking
  • Unique and eccentric personality that brings humor to dark situations
  • Compassionate leadership style and care for their comrades
  • Groundbreaking research on Titans that advances the plot
  • Gender-neutral presentation and representation
  • Complex character development from researcher to commander
  • Their ability to find beauty and wonder even in terrifying creatures
  • Unwavering dedication to understanding and protecting humanity
