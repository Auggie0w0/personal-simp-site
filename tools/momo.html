Maomao

From The Apothecary Diaries

Maomao is the protagonist of The Apothecary Diaries series. She is an illegitimate member of the La Clan who was raised in the red-light district. After being kidnapped and sold by bandits, she found herself working as a servant in the Imperial Palace of Li, where she uses her knowledge of herbs, poisons, and medicine to solve mysterious problems within the palace.

Age 16-17
Birthday Unknown
Role Apothecary
Personality Practical & Curious

Character Analysis

Maomao represents a complex character whose practical approach to life is shaped by her upbringing in the red-light district and under her adoptive grandfather's tutelage. Her exceptional knowledge of herbs, poisons, and medicine makes her a valuable asset in the Imperial Palace, despite her lower social status. While she presents herself as logical and detached, she harbors a deep sense of justice and compassion that often drives her actions. Her relationship with Jinshi showcases her struggle with emotional vulnerability, as she tends to downplay her own feelings and deny the growing connection between them.

Fun Facts

  • She has a genius-level intellect when it comes to identifying poisons and herbs
  • She often conducts dangerous experiments on herself out of curiosity
  • She paints fake freckles on her face to make herself less attractive and avoid unwanted attention
  • She was raised by her adoptive grandfather Luomen, a skilled apothecary
  • Despite her blunt demeanor, she has a strong sense of justice
  • She's known for her unhinged enthusiasm when it comes to poisons and medicinal herbs
  • She's highly observant and has exceptional deductive reasoning skills
