Roronoa Zoro

From One Piece (Live Action)

Roronoa Zoro is a skilled swordsman and the first member to join Luffy's crew in the live action adaptation of One Piece. Portrayed by Mackenyu, Zoro is a disciplined warrior with dreams of becoming the world's greatest swordsman. His distinctive green hair and three-sword fighting style make him instantly recognizable.

Age 19
Birthday November 11
Role First Mate/Swordsman
Personality Stoic & Determined

Character Analysis

Mackenyu's portrayal of Roronoa Zoro brings the iconic character to life with impressive sword skills and physical presence. His performance captures Zoro's unwavering loyalty to his captain and crew, as well as his relentless pursuit of strength. The live action adaptation maintains Zoro's core traits: his terrible sense of direction, his dedication to swordsmanship, and his unbreakable resolve.

Fun Facts

  • Played by Japanese actor Mackenyu, son of legendary martial artist and actor Sonny Chiba
  • Uses the Three Sword Style (Santoryu), holding one sword in his mouth and one in each hand
  • Has a notoriously poor sense of direction
  • Trained under Koshiro and made a promise to his childhood friend Kuina to become the world's greatest swordsman
  • His green hair was faithfully recreated in the live action adaptation
