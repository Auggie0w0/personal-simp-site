Saja

From K-pop Demon Hunters

Saja is a charismatic and powerful demon hunter from "K-pop Demon Hunters", a unique series that blends K-pop culture with supernatural action. As a skilled warrior with a magnetic personality, he battles demons while maintaining his status in the entertainment world. His character represents the perfect balance of charm, strength, and mystery in this innovative crossover series.
